cry_pmg2gui(structure, pbc=[True, True, True], symmetry=True, zconv=None)

Transform a pymatgen Structure object into a CRYSTAL structure (gui) object. Vacuum needs to be included because pymatgen only includes 3D symmetry.

Parameters:

  • structure (Structure | Molecule) – Pymatgen Structure / Molecule object.

  • pbc (list[bool]) – Periodic boundary conditions along the x, y, z directions.

  • symmetry (bool) – Do symmetry analysis.

  • zconv (list[list[int, int]]) – 1st element: The index of atom; 2nd element: The new conventional atomic number.
